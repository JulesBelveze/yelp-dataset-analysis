Description of the work on networks

We have decided two work on different networks. The first one will be focusing on interactions between users whereas the other one on businesses.

  • Network of users

    • By constructing this network our intention was to identify if the users were grouped into kind of communities. To do so, we picked up influent elite users (we chose them according to their number of friends and reviews) and we draw edges between them and their friends.

  • Network of business

    • We have built this network in order to find links between different businesses. The most relevant way of linking businesses for us was to link two businesses if the same user has left a review on both of them.

    Network of business

    Here is a quick animation where you can visualize the network of businesses as described above.You can play with two parameters: the year of the elite users that link the businesses and the possibility, or not, to view the different communities inside this network.

    Since the above layout does not help to visualize how the business are geographically located we have decided to display them with a map background. As we could have imagined business within the same area are linked. But surprisingly there is a lot of connections between business that are located in cities really far away from each other.

    Network of elite users

    Here we are focusing on the established links between users. As mentionned above you are only focusing on elite users from different years that are influent (at least 10 friends) and active (they have at least written 50 reviews). We have decided to draw this network in order pinpoint the different users communities.
    Blue nodes represent the elite users we are considering and the red ones are regular users. When on "With Communities" mode, you can easily distinguish the communities by color.
