He is a member of the [[Republican Party (United States)|Republican Party]].\n\n==Early life and education==\nHollingsworth was born in [[Clinton, Tennessee]]. He attended the [[Webb School of Knoxville|Webb School]] in [[Knoxville, Tennessee]], and the [[University of Pennsylvania]], graduating from the [[Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania|Wharton School]]. After graduating from Wharton, Hollingsworth founded Hollingsworth Capital Partners, which specialized in rebuilding old manufacturing sites and returning them to service.
