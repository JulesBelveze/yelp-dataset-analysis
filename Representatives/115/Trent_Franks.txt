Franks is the son of Juanita and Edward Taylor Franks.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://freepages.genealogy.rootsweb.ancestry.com/~battle/reps/franks.htm|title=Trent Franks ancestry|website=freepages.genealogy.rootsweb.ancestry.com}}</ref> He was born with a [[cleft lip and palate]]. He moved to Arizona in 1981, where he continued to drill wells.\n\nIn 1987, he completed a course of study at the non-accredited [[National Center for Constitutional Studies]], formerly known as the Freemen Institute, in Utah.<ref>{{Cite news|author =[[Associated Press]]| title =Primaries crowded for redrawn 2nd Congressional District| newspaper =[[Kingman Daily Miner]]| date =August 12, 2002| url =https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=NaU0AAAAIBAJ&sjid=tlIDAAAAIBAJ&pg=4877,3632056&dq=trent+franks+ottawa+university&hl=en| accessdate =March 6, 2010 }}</ref> For one year, from 1989 to 1990, he attended the Arizona campus of [[Ottawa University]], based in Ottawa, Kansas.<ref>{{cite web |publisher=Vote-USA.org |accessdate=September 29, 2009 |url=http://www.vote-usa.org/Intro.aspx?Id=AZFranksTrent |title=Trent Franks }}</ref>\n\nIn September 2004, Franks told ''Franchising World'' that he had been a small business owner for more than 25 years.
