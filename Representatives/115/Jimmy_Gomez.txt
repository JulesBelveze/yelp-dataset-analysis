He was first elected in November 2012 and reelected in 2014 with over 83% of the vote. California\u2019s 51st Assembly District includes Northeast Los Angeles and unincorporated East Los Angeles. He served as State Assembly Majority Whip from 2013 to 2014.\n\nGomez was a member of the California Latino Legislative Caucus.
