[[Meritorious Service Medal (United States)|Meritorious Service Medal]]\n}}\n'''Jeffrey John Denham''' {{IPAc-en|\u02c8|d|\u025b|n|\u0259|m}} (born July 29, 1967) is an American politician, [[United States Air Force]] veteran, and businessman. A member of the [[Republican Party (United States)|Republican Party]], he is the [[United States House of Representatives|U.S. Representative]] for {{ushr|CA|10}}. Denham first won election to the U.S. House in 2010, representing {{ushr|CA|19}} for one term before redistricting led him to run in the 10th district in 2012.\n\nFrom 2002 to 2010, Denham served in the [[California State Senate]], representing the [[California's 12th State Senate district|12th District]], which includes [[Madera County, California|Madera]], [[Merced County, California|Merced]], [[Monterey County, California|Monterey]], [[San Benito County, California|San Benito]], and [[Stanislaus County, California|Stanislaus]] counties. Prior to seeking political office, Denham served on active and reserve status in the [[United States Air Force]] for 16 years, and served in both [[Gulf War|Operation Desert Storm]] in Iraq and [[Unified Task Force|Operation Restore Hope]] in Somalia.\n\nDenham has been especially active in immigration issues, notably in the effort to draft and pass legislation that would successfully resolve the status of the so-called Dreamers. \n\n==Early life, education, and military service==\nIn 1984, at age 17, Denham enlisted in the [[United States Air Force]]. He served on active and reserve status for 16 years as a tactical aircraft maintenance specialist<ref>http://combatveteransforcongress.org/cand/2791</ref>, during which he received the [[Meritorious Service Medal (United States)|Meritorious Service Medal]] for his service during [[Operation Desert Storm]] ([[Iraq]]) and [[Operation Restore Hope]] ([[Somalia]]).<ref name=wsj>{{cite news |title=Jeff Denham (R)|url=http://projects.wsj.com/campaign2012/candidates/view/jeff-denham--CA-H|website=Election 2012|publisher=[[The Wall Street Journal]] |accessdate=November 14, 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|last1=Doyle|first1=Michael|title=Rep.
