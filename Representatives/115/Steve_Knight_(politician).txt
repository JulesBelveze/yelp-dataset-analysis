American politician of the [[Republican Party (United States)|Republican Party]] serving as the [[United States House of Representatives|U.S. Representative]] for [[California's 25th congressional district]] since 2015. Previously, he represented [[California's 21st State Senate district]] from 2012 to 2015 and [[California's 36th State Assembly district]] from 2008 through 2012. From 2010 to 2012, he served as Assistant Minority Leader in the California State Assembly.<ref>{{cite news|last1=Smith|first1=Perry|title=Speculation Begins For Steve Knight's State Senate Seat|url=http://www.hometownstation.com/santa-clarita-latest-news/speculation-begins-for-steve-knights-state-senate-seat-93233|accessdate=February 17, 2016 |publisher=KHTS |date=November 10, 2014}}</ref> Knight served in the U.S. Army from 1985 to 1993 and served for 18 years with the [[Los Angeles Police Department]]. He was previously a member of the [[Palmdale, California]] City Council.\n\n==Early life, military service and career==\nKnight was born at [[Edwards Air Force Base]] in [[Antelope Valley, California]], in 1966.<ref>{{cite web|title=KNIGHT profile|url=http://bioguide.congress.gov/scripts/biodisplay.pl?index=K000387|publisher=Biographical Directory of the United States Congress|accessdate=February 17, 2016}}</ref> After graduating from [[Palmdale High School]], he served in the U.S. Army (1985\u201387) as a track systems mechanic in [[Friedberg, Germany]].<ref>{{cite news|title=New Members Guide 2015|url=http://thehill.com/new-members-guide-2014/223672-rep-elect-steve-knight-r-calif-25|accessdate=17 February 2016|publisher=The Hill}}</ref> When his tour ended, he served in the Army Reserve until 1993.<ref>{{cite news|date=December 1, 2014 |title=New Members of the 114th Congress Who Have Served in the Military|url=http://www.ebmpubs.com/ECN_pdfs/ecn1214_News.pdf|newspaper=Exchange and Commissary News|publisher=Executive Business Media|location=Westbury, NY|page=11}}</ref> In 2006, Knight received an [[Associate degree|Associate of Arts]] degree from [[Antelope Valley College]].<ref name=rollcall>{{cite web|url=http://media.cq.com/members/53674?rc=1|title=House of Representatives Member Profile; Rep.
