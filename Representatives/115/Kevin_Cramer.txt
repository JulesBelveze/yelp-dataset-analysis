Cramer previously chaired the [[North Dakota Republican Party]] (1991\u20131993) and served as State Tourism Director (1993\u20131997) and Economic Development Director (1997\u20132000). He served on the [[North Dakota Public Service Commission]] from 2003 to 2012.\n\nCramer is the Republican nominee in the [[United States Senate election in North Dakota, 2018|2018 U.S. Senate election in North Dakota]].\n\n== Early life and education ==\nCramer was born in [[Rolette, North Dakota]], the first of five children of Clarice (Hjelden) and Richard Cramer.<ref>http://boulgerfuneralhome.com/obituaries/richard-cramer/</ref> He was raised in [[Kindred, North Dakota]], in [[Cass County, North Dakota|Cass County]]. Cramer graduated from Kindred High School. He received a B.A. degree from [[Concordia College (Moorhead)|Concordia College]] in [[Moorhead, Minnesota]], in 1983.
