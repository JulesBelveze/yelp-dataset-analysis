After initially leading in the race for the Republican nomination, he was overtaken in the polls by [[Marco Rubio]], and in April 2010, Crist left the Republican Party and ran as an [[Independent politician|Independent]].<ref name=\"online.wsj.com\">{{cite news |title=Crist Looks to Go It Alone |work=The Wall Street Journal |last=Wallsten |first=Peter; Bauerlein, Valerie |date=April 29, 2010 |url=https://www.wsj.com/news/articles/SB10001424052748704423504575212320310724084}}</ref> In the general election, he lost to Rubio in a three-way race, taking 30% of the vote to Rubio's 49% and Democratic nominee [[Kendrick Meek]]'s 20%.
