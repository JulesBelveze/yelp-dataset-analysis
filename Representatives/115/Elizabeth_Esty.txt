She is a [[Democratic Party (United States)|Democrat]]. Previously she was a member of the [[Connecticut House of Representatives]], representing the 103rd Assembly District, which consisted of [[Cheshire, Connecticut|Cheshire]] and parts of [[Hamden, Connecticut|Hamden]] and [[Wallingford, Connecticut|Wallingford]]. She also served two terms on the Cheshire Town Council. The 5th congressional district spans central and northwest Connecticut, including Waterbury, Danbury, New Britain, Meriden, Torrington, Litchfield County, the Farmington Valley, Newtown, and Esty's hometown of Cheshire.\n\nEsty defeated two challengers in the August 14, 2012, Democratic primary to become the Democratic nominee in [[Connecticut's 5th congressional district]], and on November 6, 2012, she defeated challenger [[Andrew Roraback]].
