In a letter sent to his constituents in reply to those favoring the continuation of Net Neutrality guidelines, Biggs has said that \"the repeal of net neutrality also maintains consumer and anti-competitiveness protections enforced by the FCC and the Federal Trade Commission,\" and that he \"(does) not believe that adding an extra layer of regulations will help to protect consumers.
