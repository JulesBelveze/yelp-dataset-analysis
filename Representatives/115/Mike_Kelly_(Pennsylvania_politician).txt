He has spent most of his life in [[Butler, Pennsylvania|Butler]], a city north of Pittsburgh. He played varsity football as a [[Fullback (American football)|fullback]] in high school, and his team reached two [[WPIAL]] championship games. He graduated from [[Butler High School (Butler, Pennsylvania)|Butler High School]] in 1966. He received a scholarship to play football at [[University of Notre Dame]], but his playing ended because of an injury.\n\nAfter college, he worked for his father's [[Chevrolet]]/[[Cadillac]] car dealership.
