He completed [[Naval Justice School|U.S. Naval Justice School]] in 2005. Later that year, he received orders from Trial Service Office Command South East at the [[Naval Station Mayport]], Florida, as a [[military justice|JAG prosecutor]]. In 2006, he was promoted from [[Lieutenant Junior Grade]] (LTJG) to Lieutenant (LT).
