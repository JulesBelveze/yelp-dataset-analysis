He has been a [[Democratic Party (United States)|Democratic]] member of the [[United States House of Representatives]] since 1997. He currently represents [[California's 30th congressional district]] within the [[San Fernando Valley]], in [[Los Angeles County, California|Los Angeles County]], California.\n\nSherman previously represented the [[California's 24th congressional district|24th]] and [[California's 27th congressional district|27th]] congressional districts in Los Angeles County.
